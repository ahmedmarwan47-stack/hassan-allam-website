HAP AtA Glance

Scroll

Hassan Allam Properties is the boutique real-estate arm of Hassan Allam Holding — one of Egypt's oldest and most respected engineering and construction groups, with roots reaching back to 1936.

Across three generations, the family has grown from a single construction firm into a nationwide developer, building intimate, thoughtfully designed communities where families always come first.

Since its inception in the 1990’s, Hassan Allam Properties [HAP] has carved an enviable niche for itself as an exclusive boutique developer, building intimate communities where families always come first.

Originating as a trusted family-run business passed down through three generations, HAP draws on decades of development expertise to master the equation of private luxury and inviting hospitality — going beyond just building homes.

Our Journey

1936

The Founding of Hassan Allam

Hassan Allam Group is founded in Cairo, laying the first stone of a family legacy that would grow across three generations.

1990s

A Dedicated Real Estate Arm

Hassan Allam Properties emerges as the group's boutique real estate developer, focused on intimate, family-first communities.

2006

The First Gated Community

HAP delivers its first fully gated neighborhood in New Cairo, setting the standard for the exclusive communities that would follow.

2015

A Move Toward the Coast

The portfolio expands beyond the capital to the Red Sea and North Coast, bringing HAP's family-first ethos to seaside living.

2020

A Nationwide Portfolio

With 30+ active developments across 10 governorates, HAP solidifies its place among Egypt's most trusted developers.

Today

Building for the Next Generation

Three generations in, Hassan Allam Properties keeps crafting communities where families come first — now and for decades to come.
